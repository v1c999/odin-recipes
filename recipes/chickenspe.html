Chicken Spaghetti

Total Prepare Time

10 minutes

Cook Time

15 minutes

Total Time

25 minutes

Servings

6 Servings

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Chicken
  • Canned soup
  • Canned tomatoes
  • Cheese
  • Mushrooms
  • Seasonings

Instructions

  1. Cook the pasta, drain it, then return it to the pot.
  2. Add the remaining ingredients to the pot.
  3. Cook, stirring frequently, until the cheese is melted.
