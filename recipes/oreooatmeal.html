Oreo oatmeal

Total Prepare Time:

2 minutes

Total Cook Time:

5 minutes

Total Time:

7 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup milk of choice
  • 1/2 cup old fashioned oats
  • 1 Tbsp chia seeds (optional)
  • 2-3 Oreo cookies, chopped

Instructions

  1. Heat the milk in a small saucepan on medium heat. Add in the oats and chia seeds. Bring to a boil, and reduce the heat. Allow to simmer for 4-5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  2. Pour into a bowl and top with yogurt and Oreo cookies.
